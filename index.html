School project

Skin lesion detection

In this project, I investigated the feasibility of reliably measuring specific features of skin lesions through image analysis algorithms. The goal was to develop algorithms for extracting relevant features from skin lesion images, train machine learning models for accurate classification, and assess their performance compared to human dermatologists' diagnoses. Additionally, I explored the significance of consistent image quality for model development. The study utilized a diverse dataset of skin lesion images and employed various feature extraction techniques, ultimately highlighting the potential of machine learning in medical image analysis

Netflix data exploration
with pandas

This guided project explores trends in Netflix movie durations over time using a dataset sourced from DataCamp. Through data cleaning, filtering, and exploratory data analysis in Python, I investigated whether there has been a decline in the duration of movies on the platform. The results indicate a trend towards shorter movies, although the decrease in duration is not substantial.
